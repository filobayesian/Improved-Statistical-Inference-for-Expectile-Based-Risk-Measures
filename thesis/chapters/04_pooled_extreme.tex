\chapter{Direct pooled extreme expectiles}
\label{ch:pool-ext}

\section{Working estimator}
\label{sec:pool-ext:estimator}

The rebuilt thesis starts from the direct pooled extreme-expectile
estimator
\begin{equation}
\label{eq:pool-ext:working-estimator}
  \widehat\xi_{\tau_n}^{pool,\star}
  =
  \psifn\!\big(\widehat\gamma_n(\bm\omega)\big)\,
  \widehat q_n^\star(\tau_n\mid\bm\omega),
\end{equation}
where $\widehat q_n^\star(\tau_n\mid\bm\omega)$ denotes the
weighted-geometric pooled Weissman extreme-quantile estimator in the
sense of \textcite{DaouiaPadoanStupfler2021}. The bridge to
expectiles is applied once, after quantile pooling, through
\[
  \psifn(\gamma)=(\gamma^{-1}-1)^{-\gamma}.
\]

No theorem is asserted in this chapter yet. The purpose of the current
draft is to isolate the exact algebraic objects that the source audit
and subsequent proof have to address.

\section{Exact log decomposition}
\label{sec:pool-ext:decomposition}

For the population quantile $Q(\tau_n)$ and population expectile
$\xi_{\tau_n}$, the log-relative error satisfies the identity
\begin{equation}
\label{eq:pool-ext:central-decomposition}
  \log\frac{\widehat\xi_{\tau_n}^{pool,\star}}{\xi_{\tau_n}}
  =
  \log\frac{\widehat q_n^\star(\tau_n\mid\bm\omega)}{Q(\tau_n)}
  +
  \left\{
    \log\psifn\!\big(\widehat\gamma_n(\bm\omega)\big)
    -
    \log\psifn(\gamma)
  \right\}
  -
  \log\frac{\xi_{\tau_n}}{\psifn(\gamma)Q(\tau_n)}.
\end{equation}
This identity is the new load-bearing object. The first term is the
pooled Weissman error. The second term is the additional cost of
estimating $\gamma$ inside the expectile--quantile constant. The third
term is the deterministic population error in replacing the expectile
by $\psifn(\gamma)Q(\tau_n)$.

\section{Source-audit tasks}
\label{sec:pool-ext:source-audit}

For reference, write the three terms in
\eqref{eq:pool-ext:central-decomposition} as
\[
  A_n
  =
  \log\frac{\widehat q_n^\star(\tau_n\mid\bm\omega)}{Q(\tau_n)},
  \qquad
  B_n
  =
  \log\psifn\!\big(\widehat\gamma_n(\bm\omega)\big)
  -
  \log\psifn(\gamma),
\]
and
\[
  C_n
  =
  \log\frac{\xi_{\tau_n}}{\psifn(\gamma)Q(\tau_n)}.
\]
These definitions are only notation for the exact identity
\[
  \log\frac{\widehat\xi_{\tau_n}^{pool,\star}}{\xi_{\tau_n}}
  =
  A_n+B_n-C_n.
\]

The next research note should answer the following source-audit
questions before the chapter states any theorem:
\begin{enumerate}[label=(\roman*)]
\item What exact pooled Weissman statement applies to $A_n$, and is it
      formulated for log-relative error or relative error?
\item What exact pooled Hill statement applies to
      $\widehat\gamma_n(\bm\omega)-\gamma$, and what additional
      argument is needed to pass through $\log\psifn$?
\item What exact expectile--quantile expansion applies to $C_n$, and
      under which assumptions?
\item After the first three questions are answered, what normalisation
      is actually justified for the sum $A_n+B_n-C_n$?
\end{enumerate}
