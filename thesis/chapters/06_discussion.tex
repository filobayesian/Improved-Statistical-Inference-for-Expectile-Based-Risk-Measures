\chapter{Discussion and future work}
\label{ch:discussion}

The thesis is currently in a research-reset phase. The main future work
is not an extension beyond the iid distributed setting, but the
completion of the direct pooled extreme-expectile theorem from the
decomposition in \Cref{eq:pool-ext:central-decomposition}.

Once that theorem is fixed, the remaining work is sequential: choose
weights justified by the theorem, derive the corresponding confidence
intervals, rebuild the simulation study, and then rewrite the
introduction and discussion around the validated result.
