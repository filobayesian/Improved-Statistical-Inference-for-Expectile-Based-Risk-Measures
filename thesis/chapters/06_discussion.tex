\chapter{Discussion and future work}
\label{ch:discussion}

The thesis is still in a research-reset phase, but the first rebuilt
theorem is now in place. \Cref{thm:pool-ext:deterministic} shows that,
under the conservative bridge-rate condition, the direct pooled
extreme-expectile estimator inherits the first-order pooled Weissman
limit in the iid common-marginal distributed setting.

The remaining work is sequential: choose weights justified by the
theorem, derive the corresponding confidence intervals, rebuild the
simulation study, and then rewrite the discussion around the completed
set of validated results.
